What is Birth Chart Analysis?

Birth Chart Analysis, also known as Natal Chart Analysis, is an in-depth examination of an individual's astrological chart, which is created based on the exact time, date, and place of their birth. This analysis is rooted in Vedic Astrology (or Western astrology, depending on tradition), and it maps the positions of celestial bodies such as the Sun, Moon, planets, and other astrological points at the time of birth. Key Principles of Analysis

  • Sun Sign: Represents your core self, ego, and individuality.
  • Moon Sign:Governs emotions, instincts, and unconscious mind.
  • Rising Sign (Ascendant):Determines how others perceive you and how you approach the world.
  • Nodes (Rahu & Ketu):They are linked to destiny, past life influences, and spiritual evolution.
