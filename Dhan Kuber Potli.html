What is Dhan Kuber Potli?

Dhan Kuber Potli is a sacred talisman linked to Lord Kuber, revered as the deity of wealth and prosperity in Hindu mythology. This potli (small pouch) is traditionally filled with items believed to attract wealth, such as rice, saffron, gemstones, or coins, and is considered a symbol of endless prosperity and abundance. Astrologically, it is believed to enhance financial luck, stabilize income, and ward off negative energies that hinder monetary growth.

The Dhan Kuber Potli is often kept in cash boxes, safes, or near idols of Lord Kuber in the home or workplace. It is said to work best when energized through specific rituals, mantras, and astrological guidance.
