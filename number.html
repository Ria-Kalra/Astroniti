What is Numerology?

Numerology is an ancient belief system that uses numbers to reveal deeper insights into a person's life and destiny. It is based on the idea that each number has a unique vibration and influence that affects various aspects of your life, from your personality to your future.

Key Components of Numerology

  • Life Path Number: Calculated from your birthdate, and reflects the opportunities and challenges you will face throughout your life.
  • Expression Number: Derived from the full name you were given at birth, this number represents your natural talents, skills, and the way you communicate with the world.
  • Soul Urge Number: This number, derived from the vowels in your full birth name, reveals your inner desires, motivations, and the core of your true self.
  • Personality Number: Calculated from the consonants of your full birth name, this number offers insight into how others perceive you and the way you present yourself to the world.

Benefits of Numerology

  • Alignment with Universal Energy
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Clearer Path to Success
  • Understanding Life Cycles
